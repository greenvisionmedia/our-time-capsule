About Us

We first launched in 2021 with the goal of elevating the voices of Chicago’s youth during the COVID-19 pandemic. We wanted to help them see themselves as the incredibly special generation of kids that they are – kids who were able to meet the changes and challenges of the time and emerge stronger. 

In partnership with 40 organizations, including Chicago Public Library, Chicago Children’s Theatre, Lurie Center for Childhood Resilience, MyChi MyFuture, and Multiple, we helped kids from all corners of the city contribute their stories to a citywide time capsule.

We are now thrilled to be working with kids and leaders from across the country to grow Once Upon Our Time Capsule from a pandemic response project to an organization that provides a platform for kids to document, share, and amplify their stories.
