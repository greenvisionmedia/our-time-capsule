Help power Our Time Capsule

Once Upon Our Time Capsule is a registered 501(c)(3) organization. Donations help us reach more kids, grow our storytelling platform, and produce exciting content that amplifies the voices of kids.

There are other great ways to give 

  • Checks and Grants from Donor Advised Funds. We accept checks and grants from Donor Advised Funds. Once Upon Our Time Capsule is a registered 501(c)3, EIN #88-3477844
  • Matching Gifts. Increase the impact of your gift through your company’s matching gift program. To find out if your company has a matching gift program, contact your company's human resources or corporate giving representative.
  • Gifts of Appreciated Stock and Securities. For proper donor recognition, transfers should include donor name and address, in addition to the number of security and shares.

Email us at gvm-he with any additional questions on ways to support!
