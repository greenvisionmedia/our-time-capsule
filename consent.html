Once Upon Our Time Capsule is a non-profit organization dedicated to amplifying the experiences of kids across America. In doing so, we aim to help kids build a strong sense of self, as well as build a greater sense of belonging and empathy toward kids from their community and communities across the country.

As part of programming, kids have the opportunity to contribute to a nationwide digital “Time Capsule”. The digital Time Capsule is a storytelling platform where kids can showcase their own stories in writing, art or audio and explore the stories of other kids.
